Hoop Break

Hoop Break's goal is to allow users to not only easily join and schedule games, but to help users forge meaningful relationships and learn life skills through pickup basketball. Read about our story here.

How Hoop Break works

Join games and view the availability of games near you

Customize your Baller Profile with your height, weight, preferred position, and hometown, and follow friends

Create courts and view the availability of those courts

Find or create courts to play on near you using the map
