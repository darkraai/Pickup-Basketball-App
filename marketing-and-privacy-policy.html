Instagram

Our Instagram posts aim to provide quality basketball analysis of NBA teams. Currently, we are providing an in-depth analysis of the basketball teams currently playing in the NBA bubble in Orlando, Florida. Each day we provide a discussion for a new NBA team and answer any comments that get thrown our way. This is our primary form of social media, and we hope to draw our basketball-oriented users to our app by providing them with quality content.

Twitch

Our twitch channel is our second mode of social media marketing. Here, we live stream NBA games with an NBA Playmakers License and provide commentary and a play-by-play analysis of the game. In contrast to the regular NBA commentators who merely state what is going on in the game such as pointing out a flasher dunk or three pointer, our commentary dives into a complete breakdown of the game using next-level statistics from Basketball Reference, a discussion of the strengths and weaknesses of both teams, and the different offensive and defensive gameplans/strategies the competing teams use. This provides the much-needed context for our audience to better understand what they are watching and improves their basketball game-sense.
